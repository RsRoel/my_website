A Little About InsightOwls

We believe that the most useful insights come from your customers. When your customers are online (E-commerce industry) getting those insights is not a problem. Offline it is. Our mission is to bring those insights also available for every offline company. With these insights you have the chance to go that extra mile to deliver that extra value to your customer. With our device you give your customers the chance to give their opinion on everything you need their opinion on. This with smartly formulated questions. We at InsightOwls then analyse the respones after which you can login to the easy-to-use dashboard and have a nice, clear, understandble overview of what your customers think on which you can take action on when needed.

Becoming data-driven gets more and more important in the increasingly digital world. In the current world people don't come or buy in your store without first reading reviews on the internet on your company or the specific product they want. We believe that you should have the possibilty to tap into this changing landscape to get the competitive advantadge and be able to react fast to what your customers say and think about you. With InsightOwls you have this possibility. Whether you want to know if you customers are happy with the service, or if you want to let them decide on the new chairs you wnat to buy, InsightOwls gives you the chance to become data-drive and get your customers feel more involved.
